About Us

PB Hotel Consultants is an international Hotel Management-Consulting firm, led by Pierre Berthier, graduated from the worldwide recognized Lausanne Hotel School, an expert with 30 years working in the field, from Bell Boy to general manager of various hotel chains. We are nationally and internationally recognized as one of the foremost specialists in the hospitality and tourism industries. We provide a comprehensive range of advisory services to assist in the process of planning tourism, cultural and recreational development initiatives and in developing strategic plans at regional and international levels.
